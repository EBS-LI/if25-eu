# CINEA Self-Check Questionnaire — Leitfaden

**EU Innovation Fund IF25 NZT — Small-Scale**

---

## Ueberblick

Der Self-Check Questionnaire ist ein Fragebogen von CINEA, der vor der Einreichung
ausgefuellt werden muss. Er prueft die grundsaetzliche Foerderfaehigkeit des Projekts.

**Wo zu finden:** EU Funding & Tenders Portal → Call IF25 NZT → Documents → Self-Check Questionnaire

---

## Typische Fragen und empfohlene Antworten

### 1. Project Type and Eligibility

**Frage:** Does your project fall under one of the eligible categories?
**Antwort:** Yes — Net-Zero Technologies (innovative technologies for renewable energy, energy storage, carbon capture utilization and storage)
**Begruendung:** Biogas upgrading to biomethane (renewable energy) + CCU (CO2 capture and utilization for food-grade applications)

**Frage:** Is your project's CAPEX within the eligible range (EUR 2.5-20 million for Small-Scale)?
**Antwort:** Yes — CAPEX is approximately EUR 15 million

**Frage:** Is your project located in an EU Member State, EEA country, or eligible associated country?
**Antwort:** Yes — Germany (EU Member State)

### 2. Innovation Gate

**Frage:** Does your project involve technologies or processes that are significantly innovative compared to the state of the art?
**Antwort:** Yes — The Reverion rSOC technology represents a breakthrough innovation:
- First commercial deployment of rSOC for CO2 capture from biogas off-gas
- Fundamental technology change from thermal (RTO) to electrochemical (rSOC)
- Simultaneous production of electricity and food-grade CO2
- Unique multi-site consortium integration

**Frage:** Is the innovation incremental or breakthrough?
**Antwort:** Breakthrough — The Reverion technology is a fundamentally different approach (electrochemical vs. thermal), not an optimization of existing technology.

### 3. GHG Avoidance

**Frage:** Does your project lead to significant GHG emission avoidance?
**Antwort:** Yes — Estimated 38,000 t CO2-eq/year through:
- Biomethane substituting fossil natural gas (~29,000 t/a)
- Biogenic food-grade CO2 substituting fossil CO2 (~9,200 t/a)

### 4. Financial Viability

**Frage:** Has the project identified a clear financing structure?
**Antwort:** Yes — IF Grant + BioMeth Bayern + KfW 270 + Equity

**Frage:** Is the project expected to generate revenues after the grant period?
**Antwort:** Yes — Continuous revenue from biomethane sales (EEG or market) + CO2 sales

### 5. Do No Significant Harm

**Frage:** Does the project comply with the DNSH principle?
**Antwort:** Yes — Detailed DNSH assessment provided in Annex. No significant harm to any of the 6 EU Taxonomy environmental objectives.
